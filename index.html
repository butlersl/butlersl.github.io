Welcome to King Oliver's

We’re good for the soul.

Our Menu Book an Event

King Oliver’s Creole Jazz Bar is dedicated to the King of Jazz himself, Joseph Nathan Oliver, teacher of Louis Armstrong.

Our (soft) first day open was Thursday, January 25th. Now you can come in, enjoy a cocktail, pizza, and wings in the amber lighting of our bar. We offer sixteen inch (16″) build-your-own pizzas and large chicken wings! Our dine in hours are Thursday to Saturday, 5pm to midnight. King Oliver’s will continue to cater to the hungry pizza lovers of Savannah via delivery services. Late night delivery is still possible, Thursday through Saturday. Our delivery hours begin at 12am (midnight) and end at 3am. If you’re hungry, we’ve got your back!

  • Doordash
  • UberEatd
  • SavTakeOut

King Oliver’s is designed with Savannah locals and tourist in mind. Now that we are open, you can expect a delightful atmosphere, courtesy of live jazz musicians every Sunday evening. The restaurant will serve delicious cajun dishes when the time is right. Don’t forget to order a pizza in the meantime!
